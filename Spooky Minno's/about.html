Most notably known for his future time piece "The Beginning of the End: An Essay on Techno-Decay", Spooky Minno was known for his wild exploits.
He had inherited an enormous amount of coin after his family died on a ski trip in Colorado. They were smashed by a !!ski trolley???!! and killed upon contact.
Spooky recieved word while attending a science camp in the summer of 2032. In an academically titilating environment, Spooky was in his prime. Sipping on the electricity of the experiments.
The moment that he heard the news of his parents he had only a brief moment of grief. This was truly an awful moment, but Spooky had "things" to do.
It seemed the exact wrong time to reach out to him. he had successfully completed his experiment. He was in the computer.
